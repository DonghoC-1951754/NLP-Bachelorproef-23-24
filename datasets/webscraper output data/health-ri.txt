Data Driven Health & Life Sciences: Connect, Share and Reuse

 Better health for citizens and patients by reusing health data with an integrated health data infrastructure for research, policy and innovation.



































About Health-RIHealth-RI mission is better health(care) for citizens and patients by reusing health data with an integrated health data infrastructure for research, policyand innovation.If we can predict disease progression and treatment response based on biological factors, we can offer each of us a tailored treatment. We call this personalized medicine. In the same way, we can promote health and prevent disease: personalized health.

















 Aftermovie Health-RI Conference 2023: Connecting the Dots













Our vision is based on the potential of improved data reuse. By combining data from various sources - such as clinical data and lifestyle data - a deeper understanding of the complexity of disease and health can be achieved. This, in turn, leads to the development of better treatments and products, ultimately improving the health of citizens and patients.

The European Health Data Space






The European Health Data Space (EHDS) is a proposal for a regulation from the European Commission. This regulation complements the GDPR and makes it possible to share health data on a national and European level for primary and secondary use. In addition, the EHDS gives citizens more control over their health data, the European market for EHRs and wellness applications is regulated by means of certification and the EHDS prescribes how the governance of primary and secondary use must be organized in each Member State.As Health-RI, we are closely involved in the development of the EHDS, particularly in the field of secondary use of health data. Health-RI actively collaborates with the Ministry of Health, Welfare and Sport and many stakeholders in the Netherlands and Europe to gain a clear picture of interests and shape the implementation of the regulation in the Netherlands.This website contains noteworthy news about the EHDS, Health-RI statements and activities regarding the EHDS and Frequently Asked Questions.Feel free to share your questions with Health-RI here:VideoBelow, Hugo van Haastert, former VWS attach to the European Commission, briefly explains what the EHDS entails (video in Dutch).
