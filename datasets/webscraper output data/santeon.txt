Within Santeon, seven top clinical hospitals cooperate openly with the aim of improving medical care through continuous innovation.

The Health Intelligence Platform Santeon (HIPS) is the joint data platform of the seven Santeon hospitals. In it, the hospitals can quickly exchange and compare information to research, improve care and innovate._x000b_Together, the Santeon hospitals possess a large volume of valuable data on conditions, treatments and treatment results in patients and the use of expensive drugs._x000b_By bringing this data together, analysing it and making it available through reports, healthcare professionals can more easily share experiences and results. This forms an important basis for rapidly improving and innovating care. The database also provides valuable information for scientific research.
