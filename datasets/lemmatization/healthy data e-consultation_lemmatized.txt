The idea and opinion expressed by citizen during this e-consultation feed into recommendation on how to sensitise citizen to and engage them with the reuse of their health data in a Belgian a well a European perspective. this e-consultation is a pilot project within TEHDaS primarily concentrate on the citizen of the three country organizing it, namely Belgium, France and the United Kingdom. However, it will be open to any EU citizen willing to participate. Goal of this project is thus not scientific, a the contribution that will be collected will not necessarily be representative of the sociodemographic characteristic of European citizens. The goal is simply to disseminate the informative material to a many citizen a possible and to give everyone the opportunity to let their voice be heard on the platform. Expected results: An increase in the general awareness A better understanding of the state of citizens' perceptions, belief and preference towards health data secondary use and sharing Involvement of citizen in the debate and in the search for solution
