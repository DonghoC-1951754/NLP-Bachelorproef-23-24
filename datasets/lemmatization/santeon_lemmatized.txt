Within Santeon, seven top clinical hospital cooperate openly with the aim of improving medical care through continuous innovation. The Health Intelligence Platform Santeon (HIPS) is the joint data platform of the seven Santeon hospitals. In it, the hospital can quickly exchange and compare information to research, improve care and innovate._x000b_Together, the Santeon hospital posse a large volume of valuable data on conditions, treatment and treatment result in patient and the use of expensive drugs._x000b_By bringing this data together, analysing it and making it available through reports, healthcare professional can more easily share experience and results. This form an important basis for rapidly improving and innovating care. The database also provides valuable information for scientific research. Strategic partner of ICHOM.
