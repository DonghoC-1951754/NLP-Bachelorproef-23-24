Funded by the European Commission a part of the Digital Europe Program, the Data Spaces Support Centre is aimed at the public sector and company that want to create sovereign data spaces. The Data Spaces Support Centre will explore the need of data space initiatives, define common requirement and establish best practice to accelerate the formation of sovereign data space a a crucial element of digital transformation in all areas. One of the key objective of the Data Space Support Centre (DSSC) is to establish a Network of Stakeholders aim to build a strong and innovative data ecosystem in Europe through the development of common data space in strategic economic sector and domains.
