BoardAdvisory BoardCore Strategy OrganisationsGovernance and Editorial Principles#DataSavesLivesContactWhat is Data Saves Lives?Data Saves Lives is a multi-stakeholder initiative with the aim of raising wider patient and public awareness about the importance of health data, improving understanding of how it is used and establishing a trusted environment for multi-stakeholder dialogue about responsible use and good practice across Europe.Led by theEuropean Patients Forum(EPF) and theEuropean Institute for Innovation through Health Data(i~HD) Data Saves Lives ha launched this web portal, sharing relevant information and best practice example about the use of health data and generating easy-to-use material about the basic concept related to data sharing, the data journey and the safeguard in place.All content is reviewed by an independenteditorial boardand user group, to ensure accuracy, neutrality, usability and accessibility.Our material are developed in English and our aim is that these can be easily translated and adapted for local context across Europe.Data Saves Lives ha a crucial role in exploring societal question about the us of health data and supporting stakeholder dialogue, with a view to enhancing trust and cooperation.We aim to stimulate a structured and meaningful discourse within the health data community about responsible use of data and to capture, share and discus view on other data related issues.Our vision is A Europe where trustworthy data sharing support health and scientific research to meet the need of patient and address the challenge faced by our healthcare systemsOur missionis to create a European Health Data Portal that will build awareness and understanding on:1. why data is important2. how it is used3. provide a trusted environment for dialogue about the use of health data in EuropeThe initiative is being led by amulti-stakeholder core group. EPF chair this group and host the secretariat.i~HDleads on content development.An advisory board is currently being established, comprising a wide range of health data community stakeholders. They will help to guide u on the different perspective we need to convey when explaining the us of health data.Start-up funding for the initiative ha been provided by theEuropean Federation of Pharmaceutical Industries and Associations(EFPIA) andEIT Healthas an unrestricted educational grant. The funders have no control over the content.This Data Saves Lives European initiative contributes to, and connects with, a global#DataSavesLivesmovementand the corresponding Twitter handle.For more information, please contact Gzde Susuzlu, Data Saves Lives Coordinator atgozde.susuzlu@eu-patient.eu.Back to TopSearchDisclaimerPrivacy PolicyHomeContact usSearchYour health data matter
