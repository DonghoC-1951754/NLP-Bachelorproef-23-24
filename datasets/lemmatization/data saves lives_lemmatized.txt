How can sharing your health data help others? In the last 5 years, more scientific data ha been generated than in the entire history of mankind Societies that can reliably and responsibly record and share the health data of it citizen across research community will advance the understanding of diseases, allow new medicine to be developed more quickly, and empower individual to benefit others with the same or other medical conditions.The responsible use of our health data will improve the wellbeing of citizens, make our healthcare system effective, data-driven and easier to use, speed up scientific research, and make healthcare service more sustainable in a Europe that will remain a global leader in medical innovation. Data Saves Lives is a multi-stakeholder initiative with the aim of raising wider patient and public awareness about the importance of health data, improving understanding of how it is used and establishing a trusted environment for multi-stakeholder dialogue about responsible use and good practice across Europe.Led by the(EPF) and the(i~HD) Data Saves Lives ha launched this web portal, sharing relevant information and best practice example about the use of health data and generating easy-to-use material about the basic concept related to data sharing, the data journey and the safeguard in place.All content is reviewed by an independentand user group, to ensure accuracy, neutrality, usability and accessibility. Our material are developed in English and our aim is that these can be easily translated and adapted for local context across Europe.Data Saves Lives ha a crucial role in exploring societal question about the us of health data and supporting stakeholder dialogue, with a view to enhancing trust and cooperation.We aim to stimulate a structured and meaningful discourse within the health data community about responsible use of data and to capture, share and discus view on other data related issues.Our vision is A Europe where trustworthy data sharing support health and scientific research to meet the need of patient and address the challenge faced by our healthcare systemsOur mission is to create a European Health Data Portal that will build awareness and understanding on:1. why data is important2. how it is used3. provide a trusted environment for dialogue about the use of health data in EuropeThe initiative is being led by a.
