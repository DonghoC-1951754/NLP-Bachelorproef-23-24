BelRAI is the Belgian implementation of the interRAI assessment tools. RAI stand for resident assessment instruments; these are scientifically validated instrument enabling an assessment of the degree of dependency and the care need of individuals. BelRAI is a cooperative project between the different communities, regional, and federal government of Belgium. The BelRAI system in Belgium is available to all healthcare professional and healthcare providing organisation for assessing the care need of their patients. The system applies to all person receiving healthcare in Belgium. The purpose of the system is to have a unified and standardised assessment tool to evaluate the care need of person a well a their degree of dependency.
